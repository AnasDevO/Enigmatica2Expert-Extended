## ✨ New Features


#### Difficulty_rework

* <img src="https://i.imgur.com/w33R4Ip.png" align=right> [🖇](https://github.com/Krutoy242/Enigmatica2Expert-Extended/commit/6012a35b4fab30a9347712db9596db7495348d6c)💙![](https://github.com/Krutoy242/mc-icons/raw/master/i/requious/replicator__0.png "Replicator") easier
  > - Difficulty now x10 lower affect UU cost. Now 1:1 cost is 100 difficulty (cheaper replication on less difficulty).
  > - Now there is new formula for difficulty increasing: `x⁰⋅⁶ / 1000` where `x` is replicated item cost. In a nutshell, its a very small number untill you replicating something cheaper than a bucket of UU.
  > - Add ![](https://github.com/Krutoy242/mc-icons/raw/master/i/ic2/crystal_memory__0__3d4c3242.png "Crystal Memory") tooltip about difficulty increasing.

* [🖇](https://github.com/Krutoy242/Enigmatica2Expert-Extended/commit/758b1a49535b6de8ab182f66db79f66e29dd59c9)💙![](https://github.com/Krutoy242/mc-icons/raw/master/i/appliedenergistics2/controller__0__872c8e39.png "ME Controller") now will be acquired on replication
  > Preventing exploit of replication item without increasing difficulty.

      > 
      > IC2 Tin: 0.025 EU loss per block, 512 RF/t per connector  
      > IC2 Copper: 2,048 RF/t  
      > IC2 Gold: 8,192 RF/t

      > IC2 Glass Fiber: 262,144 RF/t

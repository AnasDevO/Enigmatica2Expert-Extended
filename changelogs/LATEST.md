* <img src="https://i.imgur.com/2EDSOtU.png" align=right> [üñá](https://github.com/Krutoy242/Enigmatica2Expert-Extended/commit/17ba5b86a442694e0954aa1ea1c5fe3bd8e04dd4)‚õëÔ∏èCustom Nether Portal Spread

  > 
  > - ‚ö° *Performance is about 100 times better*: less load on TPS, no more lag spikes every 160 ticks
  > - üìè *The maximum radius of portals is 4 times bigger*: 64 blocks
  > - üÜé *Many more recipes and more varieties of blocks*: now its possible to turn blocks into different kinds of Nether Ores
  > - üîÅ *Preserving block rotations when transforming*: transforming villages now way cooler
  > - üßæ *More detailed chat messages*
  > - üî† *Localization to Ru or Chinese languages*
  >

  > 
  > Details of how the portal works will be described in the Quest Book in the future.
  > 
  > If you are server admin, use `/portal_spread status` command to see all working portals.
